🌱 How to Get Soil Test Values
  1. Visit the nearest Agricultural Soil Testing Laboratory.
  2. Collect a small soil sample (about 500g) from your field.
  3. Submit the sample to the lab for testing.
  4. The lab will provide values for:
    • Nitrogen (N)
    • Phosphorus (P)
    • Potassium (K)
    • Soil pH
  5. You can also register online through the Government portal:

    🔬 Open Government Soil Test Portal

Tip: If you don't know soil values, you can use the "AI estimation option" in this form.
